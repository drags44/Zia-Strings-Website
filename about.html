About Me

Hello, I'm Ahmed Siddiqui, founder of Zia Strings. My passion for traditional attire and quality craftsmanship led me to create a brand dedicated to providing elegant, comfortable, and high-quality thobes for everyone.

At Zia Strings, we believe that clothing should be both beautiful and functional. Each piece is thoughtfully designed and tailored to ensure the perfect fit and feel.

Thank you for supporting a small business with big dreams. Please explore our collection and feel free to reach out with any questions!
